About Me

I'm an Entrepreneur at Heart...

I've always loved the idea turning an idea into a reality, so when I realized how much Police Officer were struggling with getting good training I did just that. In March 2021 Virtual Training Solutions was born, a company aimed at solving the world's problems through VR training, our first product Academy. A training program for police officers to help them experience difficult situations before they really happen. It took us a bout a year of development before we finished the product. It turns out the cost of upkeep was more then our customers could afford. This experience taught me something I'll never forget and has set me on a path to pursuing a career where I can eventually work on these ideas again.

During my time I've educated student on the needs of amputees, orginized fundraising, and planned trips around the world. Here is my favorite trip!
